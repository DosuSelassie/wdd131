Accra, Ghana

Accra is the capital city of Ghana, located along the Gulf of Guinea on the western coast of Africa. Known as Ghana's economic and administrative hub, Accra is bustling with vibrant culture, historical landmarks, and diverse communities. The city is home to iconic sites like the Kwame Nkrumah Mausoleum, Independence Square, and lively marketplaces such as Makola Market. Accra also serves as an essential gateway for commerce, tourism, and cultural exchange within West Africa.
